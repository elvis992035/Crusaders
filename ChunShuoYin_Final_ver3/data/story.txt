Lucifer, who had been the Archangel in the heaven, was very arrogant and conceited. After she got the power, she started to  disobey the rules of the heaven and the order of the God. Finally, Lucifer brought lots of angel fall down to the hell and became a devil. 

God was very very angry after he got this news. Thus, he decided to send the other Archangel - Uriel to crusade the betrayer.
